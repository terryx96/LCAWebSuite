Lambda Chi Alpha Annual Scholarship


The Lambda Chi Alpha Annual Freshman Scholarship is given to either a freshman or recent transfer student who demonstrates a commitment to the seven Core Values of that fraternity: loyalty, duty, respect, service and stewardship, honor, integrity, and personal courage. The chapter will award at least one $500 or greater scholarship per year with additional awards possible for runners up.

More information regarding the scholarship for the academic year of 2021-2022 will be available soon!


Jake Diamond, President - Meschach Melton, scholarship recipient 2020
